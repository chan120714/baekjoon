### 문제 설명

<p>In the beginning of a game, there are r red chips and g green chips on the desk. Two players A and B move alternately, starting with A and according to the following rules: If it’s your move, you choose a color and remove k chips of this color from the desk. However, k must be a divisor of the actual number of chips of the other color. You win if you remove the last chip from the desk. Your task is to ﬁnd which player can force to win this game.</p>

### 입력 

 <p>Input ﬁle contains two numbers – r and g (1 ≤ r,g ≤ 10<sup>9</sup>) – separated by a space.</p>

### 출력 

 <p>Output ﬁle should contain ”A player wins” if player A can force game to win and ”B player wins” otherwise.</p>
